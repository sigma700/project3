ROASTED GOAT RIBS

Hello there next on the list is a common dish of african origin. Lets look at the building blocks of this dish as following a standard chronollogical order

INGREDIENTS

  • Goat ribs ( of course)
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings like rosemary, basil, cayenne, paprika, soy sauce, ginger,
    lemon juice ETC

How to prepare this

  • Marinate the goat ribs in a mixture of the seasonings and oil.
  • Rub the mixture into the goat ribs until well coated.
  • Let the meat marinate in the fridge.
  • Roast the goat ribs.

You can also braise or stew goat meat in liquid using a slow cooker or pressure cooker
